What is BMI?

The body mass index (BMI, BMI) is a value that allows you to assess the degree of correspondence between a person's weight and height, and therefore indirectly assess whether the weight is insufficient, normal or excessive. Important when determining the indications for the need for treatment. Results may not be accurate for professional athletes.
