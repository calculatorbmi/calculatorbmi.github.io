Determining the ideal weight in children and adolescents

Determining the ideal weight in children and adolescents is more complex than in the case of adults, because they are constantly developing and growing. There is no single value considered "ideal" for all children, as factors such as age, sex, height and stage of development vary significantly between individuals.
